## [Pizza Sales Data Analysis](https://github.com/indradityaa/Pizza-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard)

### Project Overview

An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing pizza sales performance to understand revenue trends, customer ordering behavior, and product popularity.

### Key KPIs & Analysis

* Total Revenue & Average Order Value
* Total Orders & Total Pizzas Sold
* Daily & Hourly Order Trends
* Sales Distribution by Pizza Category & Size
* Top 5 Best-Selling & Bottom 5 Worst-Selling Pizzas
